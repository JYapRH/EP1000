Name: Yap Roe Hui Josephine

Project Title: Mood Lamp

What does it do?

This lamp is able to illuminate a room simply by waving your hand across the sensor. It is also able to change colours in the same way.

What are it's features?

- It is able to change colours by waving your hand

- An acrylic design that looks like it it being lit up

What will you make?

CAD Design, Graphics Design on the acrylic, Wooden box casing
Laser cutting Acrylic, Wooden box for casing, Acrylic supports
3D Printing Neopixel mounting, touch switch case
Micro-controller Arduino UNO/ NANO compatible
Input devices IR Sensory Module
Output devices 6 unit Neopixel WS2812 string
