Vector Graphics

Vector Graphics use mathematical functions and are characterized by clean lines. It allows for the creation of vector art. I used InkScape to trace the outline of an animal to form a vector art and create a black and white logo out of that vector art.

Step 1: Choose an image of an animal to make into vector art

Step 2: Put the image into inkscape and change lower the opacity to make it easier to see what you are drawing

Step 3: Trace over the image by using the draw bezier curves and straight lines tool and fill it in

Step 3: Get rid of the background image and add any finishing details

From computer graphics, I have learnt a lot about how to use GIMP as well as Inkscape to design and edit photos which I am sure will be a useful skill for me in the future.
